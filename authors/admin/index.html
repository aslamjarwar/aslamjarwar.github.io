Dr. Aslam Jarwar

Jan 1, 0001
I am a Senior Lecturer (Assistant Professor) at the Department of Computing, Sheffield Hallam University (SHU) and co-investigator for the Secure Ontologies for IoT Systems (SOfIoTS) project. I received recognition as a Fellow of the Higher Education Academy (FHEA), UK, and I am a Senior Member IEEE and a member of the 4-Dimensionalism Special Interest Group (4DSIG) Network. I was a Research Fellow for Industrial/Cyber-Physical System Security Modelling at the Department of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Public Policy (STEaPP), University College London (UCL), where I worked with Prof. Jeremy Watson. I was a Postdoctoral Researcher for the multidisciplinary project at the University of Manchester (UoM) and the University of Southampton (UoS), where I worked with Prof. Mark Elliot and Prof. Age Chapman. While working at UoM and UoS, I reviewed and developed provenance and ontological methods to model the security and privacy of sensitive streaming data.

I received the Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Ilyoung Chong from the Department of Information Communications Engineering, Hankuk University of Foreign Studies, South Korea. I did the MS in Information Science and Technology from the Department of Computer Sciences, Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad, Pakistan.

My research focuses on the Industrial Internet of things (IIoT)/Cyber Physical Systems Security Modelling, IoT-enabled Context-aware data processing, Semantic reasoning, and Artificial Intelligence. I am an Editorial Board Member of IASC - Intelligent Automation & Soft Computing Journal and the Managing Guest Editor for special issue on “Role of Disruptive Technologies in Modern Distribution Grid Automation” in Sustainable Energy Technologies and Assessments Journal and TPC for 4th INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON SUSTAINABLE TECHNOLOGIES FOR INDUSTRY 4.0. Previously I was TPC for IEEE WCNC 2020, NCETIET-2020, IMTIC18, IEEE global communications conference (GLOBECOM), and IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems (ICDCS). I regularly review articles for IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering, IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems, IEEE Sensors Journal, Future Generation Computer System (FGCS), and other IEEE and Springer Q1 journals. Please get in touch with me if you are interested in my research and want to collaborate or pursue the Ph.D./Master’s degree.
